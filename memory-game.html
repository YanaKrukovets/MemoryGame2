Rules
In this game you have four different circles, each with a unique colour and sound which is activated when pressed. The sound and color aids the player in remembering the sequence and the game ends if the player misses a step in the sequence.
  1. Click "Start".
  2. Wait and remember the sequence of glowing circles.
  3. Tap on the circles in accordance with the remembered chain.
  4. Have fun and train your brain.
About
Human memory is inextricably linked with the brain and thought processes. The more information we hold in memory, the more activity in the brain, which has to process this information. This is how the brain learns and works better. Research also shows that regular memory training increases IQ in adults and can improve memory in older adults and return it to those of middle age.
